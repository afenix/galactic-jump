In the beginning...

As far as the eye can see, the land of the flurps is covered alternately in smooth pink rock and the glistening, pulsating piles of flurpkind.
{{lifeForms.flurpName}} was snuggled peacefully on the outskirts of his favorite pile, when an unusual sucking noise caught the attention of all the nearby flurps. Now, sucking noises are not particularly uncommon on flurponia, but this was more of a giant whooshing noise. Everyone excitedly opened their eyes on the side of the pile that the noise was coming from, and saw a strange light emminating from what seemed to be a very big new flurp friend. It was round like a flurp, but it did not seem to have any armlegs like a flurp. It's mouthbelly was turned towards the pile, so noone could see its headbody. Still, the flurps became very excited and rushed to welcome the newcomer with Many open armlegs.
You are the closest, and the first to reach your new friend, so, as is customary for greeting a stranger in flurponia, you throw your arms around them and.... Instead of finding a mouthbelly to hug and suckle, your armfeet close around your own body. You are swept into the air and drawn into the mouthbelly of your new friend.
This is not an uncommon occurance on flurp. Sometimes, a flurp stops flurping, and the pile sucks them into their mouthbellies. Othertimes, a smallish flurp will drop out of a mouthbelly and begin to flurp. So sliding in and out of a mouthbelly is really no concern to a flurp at all. In fact, generally, nothing is of concern to a Flurp.
This mouthbelly you are being drawn into is decidedly different than what you expected. Instead of the ecstasy of melting into your new friend, you feel like you are moving very very fast through space inside of what looks like a long, shiny tube. In the direction you are moving, you see the tube spilt into 3 different directions.
Now, flurps are awfully sensitive and can feel things very deeply. When you see the tube for going very very fast in space split up, you feel different things coming from each direction.

  • From the first direction, you can feel intelligence and heavy furryness...

  • From the second, you can feel adventure and slight fuzziness...

  • From the third, you can feel warmth and hardness...
